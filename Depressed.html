Description:

The theme of this virtual room is depression. The concept is to visualize the innermost feelings of depression. I made this virtual space into a closed sphere to symbolize the patient's inner world. The models in the room are from Sketchfab. The smiling mask in the room symbolizes the mask worn by the patient to conceal the confusion in their minds. The eyes symbolize the gaze and pressure from others. The broken mirror symbolizes its dilapidated heart. Mirrors are usually used to organize or observe one's appearance. But the patient's mind has been shattered, so the mirror in the patient's inner world is also broken. Another complete mirror in the room is used to connect the patient's inner world and real life, so the video in this mirror symbolizes the patient's real world.
