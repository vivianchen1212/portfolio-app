Description:

When I have a longer vacation, I often don't know how to arrange my vacation. And I often end up wasting the whole vacation staying at home. Thus, I made this simple randomizer to help me decide which activities I am going to do for my next vacation. Enter any date or time in the boxes below and click "Get plan(s)" to randomly match an activity for the time you entered. All the collages contained in the webpage are made by myself. The original materials used to make the collages are from Unsplash.
